Our Coaches

Derek Saenz Program Director

Derek Saenz is the head coach of the boys and girls volleyball programs at Malibu High School. With over 20 years coaching at the collegiate, club, and high school levels, he enters his 2nd season at Malibu HS. He began his coaching career at his Alma mater, Granada Hills High, where he coached from 2000-2002. He coached club at Santa Monica Beach Volleyball Club (2002-2004), then started Shock Volleyball Club in Thousand Oaks in 2004. In 2008 Derek served as an assistant coach at West Chester University in Pennsylvania, eventually returning to Southern California in 2010 to coach and at Mizuno Long Beach. Derek is also Men's and Women's head coach for club sand at Pepperdine University, as well as the director of Malibu Volleyball Club.
